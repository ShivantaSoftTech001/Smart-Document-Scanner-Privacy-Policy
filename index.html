Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

Smart Document Scanner ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Camera Permission

Our app requires camera access to scan and capture documents. The camera is used only for scanning documents and images. We do not collect or upload your scanned documents to any server.

2. Document Storage

All scanned documents and generated PDF files are stored locally on your device. Users have full control over their documents and can delete them anytime from within the app.

3. Analytics Information

Our app uses Google Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This may collect anonymous information such as device type, usage statistics, and app performance data. This information does not personally identify users.

4. Crash Reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics to monitor and fix app crashes. Crash reports may include device model, operating system version, and technical error logs. No personal documents or scanned images are included in crash reports.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data or scanned documents with any third parties.

6. Data Security

Your scanned documents remain stored on your device. We do not store or upload your files to our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
